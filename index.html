The Process

The Process

I try to work according to a process with definite steps to ensure quality is not being compromised on and that the design looks beautiful even before completion.

01

Research

I look into the necessary texts and information available to lay out the requirements of a project. This may include the technologies used or information on the company background.

02

Prototyping

In this stage, the design is repeatedly constructed and mockups are created to the requirements of the client. Each iteration gets rid of problems, bugs and improves design.

03

Deployment

Final touches are made and compatibility tested. The website is deployed to the servers and tested for proper functioning.
